Lion

The heaviest lion ever recorded weighed 272kg, a male lion in Mount Kenya.

  • Scientific Name: Panthera leo
  • Average Length: The length of a female is typically between 4.6 and 5.7 feet, while the length of a male is between 5.6 and 8.3 feet.
  • Average Lifespan: Female lions have an average lifespan of about 15-16 years in the wild, while males live 8-10 years
  • Habitat: The lion inhabits grasslands, savannahs, and shrublands.

The lion is a large cat of the genus Panthera, native to Africa and India. It has a muscular, broad-chested body; a short, rounded head; round ears; and a hairy tuft at the end of its tail.
